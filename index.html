💙 Support real-world causes

Three creator-led fundraisers started by MrBeast and Mark Rober, each tackling a different real-world problem with a simple "$1 = one unit of impact" pitch. Every goal below was actually hit — and every link is still live for donations today.

🌳

#TeamTrees

$1 = one tree planted

Launched in 2019, #TeamTrees hit its original 20-million-tree goal in just 56 days. The Arbor Day Foundation is still planting, and the site still takes donations daily.

24M+ trees funded so far
🌳 Donate to #TeamTrees
🌊

#TeamSeas

$1 = one pound of trash removed

The ocean-cleanup follow-up, funding The Ocean Cleanup and Ocean Conservancy. The $30M goal was hit and the formal campaign wrapped in 2024 — but donations are still open.

34M+ lbs removed so far
🌊 Donate to #TeamSeas
💧

#TeamWater

$1 = clean water for one person, one year

The newest of the three (August 2025), partnered with WaterAid to fund lasting clean-water infrastructure for 2 million people. The $40M goal was hit in just 31 days.

$40M+ raised, 2M people reached
💧 Donate to #TeamWater

These are independent causes we're highlighting, not run by this page — Wild Pages has no affiliation with #TeamTrees, #TeamSeas, #TeamWater, the Arbor Day Foundation, The Ocean Cleanup, Ocean Conservancy, or WaterAid.
